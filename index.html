MATRIX Business Development Ltd (Matrix) is a consulting firm providing strategic and technical supports for private sector development, project monitoring & evaluation, sectoral mapping, agribusiness, value chain strengthening, supply chain management, subsector studies, improving competitiveness of the SMEs and service providers, training, capacity building, compliances in agriculture, and demand driven services in SAARC countries, Malaysia, and Singapore in various projects and organizations since 2003. Matrix Business Development Ltd is the first Licensed GLOBALG.A.P. Farm Assurer in Bangladesh. Matrix is an ISO 9001:2015 certified company in Bangladesh.
